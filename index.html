About

GoFaux tans was born out of the idea to provide a safe and affordable airbrushed alternative to tanning. GoFaux tans makes luxury spray tans affordable for college students. GoFaux tans' first location was at the University of Colorado Boulder in the Fall of 2016, and a short year later expanded to Penn State University, which is opening in the Fall of 2017. Interested in learning more, or want to bring GoFaux to your campus? Connect with on of our ambassadors!
